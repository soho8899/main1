How to make Pancakes - Message


Everyone loves pancakes! This is a Dutch pancake (or ‚ÄòPannenkoeken‚Äô in Dutch) recipe. They are a cross between an American pancake and a French cr√™pe. Honestly of all the hundreds of pancake recipes out there, this is by far THE EASIEST I have come across! Serve them with bacon, cheese, ham, jams, maple syrup, whipped cream or peanut butter! Spoil Mum, Dad and especially kids with something super special, something homemade, something so simple, yet so spectacular, this is bound to impress everyone!

Ingredients for family of 4


[ ] 3 cup floor
[ ] 3 cup milk
[ ] 3 eggs
[ ] Sugar (1 tbsp), salt (1tsp), cinnamon (1tsp)

Method


1. Mix flour, salt, sugar and cinnamon in a bowl. Make a little well in the middle and add the eggs and while whisking, add milk.
2. Switch to using a hand blender and mix. Add the oil and mix till you've got a smooth batter. Cover the batter with plastic wrap and set aside at room temperature for 30 minutes.
3. Put a frying pan on the stove at medium heat and add butter to a pan. Using a ladle, pour pancake batter into the pan. While pouring the batter, make sure you tilt the pan so you'll cover the entire bottom of the pan and get a thin pancake.
4. When the pancake starts to dry out on top the pancake can be flipped. Bake the pancake till golden on both sides.
5. Continue baking pancakes till the batter is finished.
6. Serve the pancakes hot or cold with any topping of your choosing. Put the topping on the pancake, roll it up and it's ready to be eaten.








Spaghetti Bolognese


Spaghetti Bolognese - Message


Spaghetti Bolognese is an easy and super delicious Italian classic with a meaty, chilli sauce. It can be made in less than 30 minutes with easy to prepare ingredients. This recipe is best for a family mid week dinner. Teens can easily prepare this while waiting for parents to come home from work.

Ingredients for family of 4


[ ] 500g spaghetti (1 packet)
[ ] 500g minced beef (1 packet)
[ ] 400g diced tomatoes (1 can)
[ ] 1 cup mixed vegatables
[ ] 1 onion, chopped
[ ] 2 garlic cloves, chopped
[ ] Oil (1tbsp), salt (1tsp), pepper (1tsp), worchester sauce (2tsp) , chilli sauce (1tbsp), herbs (1tbsp).
[ ] Grated parmesan cheese to serve

Method


1. Put a large saucepan or wok on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil.
2. Add garlic and onion and fry for 5 minutes.
3. Add minced beef and fry for 5 minutes.
4. Add mixed vegetables, diced tomatoes and remaining ingredients. Stir, bring to a simmer. Stirring occasionally,
5. Cook the spaghetti.
6. Drain the spaghetti, serve the bolognese sauce and parmesan cheese on top .








Ondeh Ondeh


Ondeh Ondeh - Message


This traditional ondeh ondeh recipe is made from glutinous rice flour and is easy to prepare. Ondeh ondeh (also spelled onde onde) is a popular sweet treat in Malaysia and Indonesia. Its a delicios glutinous rice balls snack/dessert. Kids will enjoy making this popular and easy snack.

Ingredients


[ ] 250g of glutinous rice flour
[ ] 200ml of pandan juice ( or coconut juice as an alternative)
[ ] 100g of grated coconut (mixed with 1tsp salt)
[ ] 150g of palm sugar, finely chopped
[ ] Salt to taste

Method


1. First, boil some water in a pot.
2. Mix the glutinous rice flour with pandan juice in a large bowl.
3. Knead lightly until well combined. Then, pinch a small piece of the dough (approximately 40g) and drop it in boiling water.
4. Once the dough rises to the surface, remove it using a slotted spoon and shake off the excess water.
5. Mix the boiled dough into the main dough and knead well. This will form a smooth dough.
6. Pinch a small piece of dough and flatten lightly with your fingers. Fill the centre with palm sugar. Pinch the edges and roll the dough in your palms to form a smooth ball.
7. Set aside until all the ingredients are used up.
8. Drop the ondeh ondeh balls into the pot of boiling water. Once they float, remove them using a slotted spoon and shake off the excess water.
9. Coat them with the grated coconut and serve!









Pork Fried Rice


Pork Fried Rice - Message


This quick and simple fried rice is sure to please the whole family. Health and delicious, it combines a thai style sauce with the fresh flavours of cabbage and broccoli. If you happen to have leftovers, its great for lunch the next day.

Ingredients


[ ]1.5 cup basmatic rice
[ ]500 pork mince
[ ]1 green cabbage, 350g broccoli, 2 carrots, 2 spring onion
[ ]2 garlic clove, 2 ginger, 4 eggs
[ ]6 tbsp oyster sauce, 2 tbsp sriracha sauce

Method


1. Cook rice. In a pot, add 3 cups of water to a boil and stir in the rice. Simmer for about 15 mins or until all of the water is absorbed. Cover the pot and turn off the heat.
2. Prepare the ingredients. Cut garlic, ginger, carrot, broccoli, cabbage and spring onion into smaller pieces.
3. Cook eggs. Heat a fry pan over medium heat, crack eggs into pan and fry until cooked to your liking.
4. Cook mince. To a large wok over medium heat, add 1-2tbsp oil. Add pork mince and stir fry for 5 mins until browned and cooked. Set aside in a bowl.
5. Cook vegetables. Using the same wok, add a little oil, add ginger, garlic, carrot, broccoli and stir fry for 2-3 min until they are tender. Stir in cabbage and cook for one min.
6. Add rice, pork and oyster sauce into the wok with the vegetables and stir until well coated. Season with salt and pepper. Divide fried rice and serve with eggs and sriracha sauce to taste.










Prawn Pasta


Prawn Pasta - Message


Pasta and Prawns. Add a little wine, fresh tomatoes, garlic and a little parmesan. Boom, you have a dinner that adds a touch of class with ease and simplicity. Sometimes the simple things in life really are the best.

Ingredients


[ ]400g spaghetti
[ ]600g prawns
[ ]4 tbsp butter
[ ]4 garlic cloves, 300g cherry tomatoes, ¬Ω cup white wine
[ ]3 spring onion, 1 small bunch parsley, 60g parmesan, 1 red chilli

Method


1. Prepare the ingredients. Heat a large pot of water on high for the pasta. Trim the spring onion and thinly slice, separately the white bottoms and green tops. Peel and mince garlic. Cutty cherry tomatoes in half. Pick the parsley leaves and chop. Slice chilli thinly.
2. Cook tomatoes. Add butter to a large deep sided pan over medium heat. Once hot, add the white bottom of the spring onion, tomatoes and garlic. Cook and stir occasionally for 2 minutes or until tomatoes releasing their juices.
3. Cook prawns. Add wine and the prawns to the pan. Season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir for 5 minutes or until cooked through. Turn off the heat.
4. Cook pasta. Add the pasta to the pot of boiling water, stirring gently until al dente. Drain the cooked pasta.
5. Add the drained pasta to the pan with prawns and fold through with the rest of spring onions, some parsley. Divide and garnish with parmesan, chilli and remaining parsley.











Slow Cooked Beef Bourguignon


Slow Cooked Beef Bourguignon - Message


The slow cook beet bourguignon will melt in your mouth with tender beef and hearty veggies cooked in a rich sauce. This meal is comforting and perfect for cold evenings. This is a slow cook recipe so it must be started in the morning for 8 hours or cooked in an instant pot for 1 hour.

Ingredients


[ ]560g chick beef diced
[ ]300g sliced speck bacon
[ ]600g potatoes, 220g mushrooms,
[ ]2 carrots, 4 garlic cloves, 280g onions
[ ]180g red wine, 140g tomato paste,
[ ]2 tbsp chicken stock, 2tbsp cornflour
[ ]1 small bunch thyme

Method


1. Prepare Ingredients. Cut carrots, mushrooms, onions, garlic, bacon into 1-2cm.
2. Brown meat. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add bacon and beef and sear for 3-4 minutes or until beef is browned and bacon starts to crisp. Add to slow cooker.
3. Make sauce. Add cornflour to half cup water and stir to combine. In the same pan, add the red wine, tomato paste and cornflour mixture. Stir until smooth and thickened slightly. Add them to the slow cooker.
4. Add the carrots, mushrooms, onions, garlic, thyme and potatoes to the slow cooker and give it a good stir. Turn the slow cooker on and cover for 8 hours or one hour if using an instant pot.
5. Turn off the slow cooker and give the contents a good stir. Serve it with rice, pasta, polenta or just on its own.
