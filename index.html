Welcome to M Child Care

Mcc was founded in 1993 on a vision of giving children the best possible start in life through access to quality early learning. We understand the level of trust you place in us when you enrol your child, and we are committed to providing the highest standards of care and education possible, with experienced and qualified educators and using the best high quality facilities.




Large Outdoor Play Area

Our centre is fortunate to have three large outdoor play areas which are all shaded by trees and additional shade cloth. Inside there are numerous toys, games and books to keep the children learning via play.

Creative Activities

There is so much to keep our little ones busy. We incorporate various sensory play, water play, cooking and art activities. Educators try to use recycled paper and items in these activities and experiences.

Learning Through Fun

When you’re having fun, you boost your positive emotions which activate the learning centres in your brain. Whether you’re playing in a team or one-to-one, you develop your social skills through play.

Nutritious Meals

Feed Australia Menu is on 4 week rotation. 8 week rotation winter and summer men. Baby vegetables and other food cooked on the premises. We discourage suger/sweer foods.
